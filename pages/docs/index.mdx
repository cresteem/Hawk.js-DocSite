# Introduction

Welcome to the Hawk.js Documentation Site!

Whether you're just starting out or you're a seasoned developer, our documentation will guide you through using Hawk.js to boost your website’s SEO and improve its visibility across search engines.

## What is Hawk.js?

Hawk.js is an advanced, open-source sitemap generator and SEO automation tool that simplifies the SEO process for web developers, SEO professionals, and digital marketers. It automates sitemap creation and submission to major search engines, including Google, Bing, Yahoo, Yandex, and all other search engines supporting IndexNow.

Key benefits include:

- **Automated Sitemap Creation**: Easily generate and maintain sitemaps with minimal manual effort.
- **Optimized Search Engine Indexing**: Ensure your website is indexed quickly and accurately, improving its visibility and search engine ranking.
- **Streamlined Submissions**: Hawk.js automates the process of submitting your sitemaps to major search engines, resulting in faster indexing.

By handling complex SEO tasks, Hawk.js lets you focus on creating quality content and enhancing your website’s features.

## Key Features of Hawk.js

1. **Automated Sitemap Generation**: Automatically generate sitemaps with ease, saving time and reducing manual work.
2. **SEO Optimization**: Hawk.js helps ensure that your site is correctly indexed by search engines, boosting visibility and improving rankings.
3. **Real-Time Sitemap Updates**: Keep your sitemaps current, reflecting the latest changes to your website content.
4. **Highly Customizable Configurations**: Tailor Hawk.js to meet your specific needs with a variety of configuration options.
5. **Multi-Search Engine Support**: Submit your sitemaps to multiple search engines, including <a href="https://google.com/">Google</a>, <a href="https://bing.com/">Bing</a>, <a href="https://www.yahoo.com/">Yahoo</a>, <a href="https://duckduckgo.com/">DuckDuckGo</a>, <a href="https://yandex.com/">Yandex</a>, <a href="https://www.naver.com/">Naver</a>, <a href="https://www.seznam.cz/">Seznam.cz</a>, and <a href="https://yep.com/">Yep</a> and others supporting IndexNow, for global reach.

## Why Use Hawk.js?

- **Save Time and Effort**: Automate the repetitive tasks of generating and submitting sitemaps, freeing you up for more important work.
- **Enhance Your SEO Performance**: Ensure faster and more accurate indexing, making it easier for search engines to discover your content and improve rankings.
- **Full Customization**: Fine-tune your sitemap and submission strategies to include or exclude specific pages, tailoring the process to your needs.
- **Up-to-Date with SEO Standards**: Hawk.js stays current with the latest SEO trends, including support for IndexNow, ensuring your website stays competitive in the search engine results.

## What's New in Version 1.5.0?

The latest version of Hawk.js (1.5.0) introduces several exciting updates and improvements:

- **Official Hawk.js Logo**: A new logo has been integrated for brand recognition.
- **TypeScript Support**: The `init` command now allows for TypeScript-based configuration, making it easier for developers to manage their setups.
- **Updated Configuration Format**: The configuration format has shifted from JSON to JavaScript for improved flexibility and ease of use.
- **Expanded Search Engine Strategies**: Additional indexing strategies such as Google Webmaster Tools and IndexNow have been added to enhance search engine compatibility.
- **Enhanced Performance**: Performance optimizations have been made throughout the CLI and internal functions for better usability and speed.

For further details, check out the [release notes](https://github.com/cresteem/Hawk.js/releases/tag/1.5.0).
